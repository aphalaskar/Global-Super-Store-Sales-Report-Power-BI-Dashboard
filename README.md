# Global-Super-Store-Sales-Report-Power-BI-Dashboard
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance for the Global Super Store dataset. The report highlights trends across regions, segments, product categories, sub‑categories, and payment modes. It is designed to help users understand sales drivers, profitability patterns, and customer purchasing behavior.
 Key Visuals & Insights
1. Sales by Payment Mode
A horizontal bar chart displaying sales contribution from different payment methods:
COD
Online
Card
This helps identify the most preferred payment modes among customers.

2. Sales by Region
A donut/pie‑style visualization showing sales distribution across global regions:
Asia
EMEA
LATAM
US/Canada
This provides a snapshot of regional performance and highest‑revenue markets.

3. Sub‑Category by Quantity & Profit
A combination bar chart showcasing product sub‑categories ranked by:
Sales quantity
Profit (positive and negative)
Green bars indicate profitable categories, while red bars highlight losses.
This helps identify product groups that drive growth or need pricing/inventory adjustments.

4. Sales by Segment
A segmented bar chart showing revenue contribution from:
Consumer
Corporate
Home Office
Useful for understanding customer demographics and segment performance.

5. Sales by Category
Visual breakdown of overall sales by:
Technology
Office Supplies
Furniture
This high-level view identifies the strongest product categories.

6. Sales by Sub‑Category
A detailed bar chart listing sub‑categories such as:
Phones
Chairs
Storage
Accessories
Bookcases
…and more
This helps pinpoint top‑performing and low‑performing sub‑categories.

Purpose of the Dashboard
Regional sales performance assessment
Product-level profitability analysis
Tracking customer segment contribution
Identifying top-selling and non-performing items
Understanding business trends using dynamic slicers (Date, Year, Month, Quarter)
